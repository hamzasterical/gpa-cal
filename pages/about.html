About GPA CAL

GPA CAL is a simple, student-friendly tool designed to help you calculate and plan your academic progress. Whether you are a university student keeping track of your current GPA, a BSCS student at UET following a specific grading system, or someone aiming for a dream CGPA, this website has you covered.

Main Features:

  • General GPA Calculator: Input your courses, credit hours, and grades to instantly compute your GPA using the 4.0 scale.
  • BSCS@UET Custom GPA: Tailored for UET Lahore BSCS students, using the official HEC grading policy and local variations in GPA calculation.
  • Dream CGPA Planner: Enter your current GPA, credit hours, and target CGPA to find out what grades you need in upcoming semesters.

Why Use GPA CAL?

Instead of manually calculating GPA or searching for formulas, GPA CAL provides quick, accurate results with a clean interface. It also supports:

  • Letter grade to GPA conversions
  • Custom credit hour assignments
  • Both semester-wise and cumulative GPA tracking

Future Plans:

We plan to expand GPA CAL to support:

  • More university-specific grading systems
  • Saving and exporting GPA reports
  • Mobile-friendly performance tracking
