Grade Point Average (GPA) is a standard measure of a student's academic performance at UET Lahore. It is calculated by averaging the grade points earned in each course, weighted by the number of credit hours. While grading systems can differ across universities and departments, UET follows an HEC-approved letter grading system. This calculator supports both letter grades and numerical marks, converting them into grade points using UET’s official scale, as shown below.

A+ = 4.3 grade points
A = 4 grade points
A- = 3.7 grade points
B+ = 3.3 grade points
B = 3 grade points
B- = 2.7 grade points
C+ = 2.3 grade points
C = 2 grade points
C- = 1.7 grade points
D+ = 1.3 grade points
D = 1 grade point
D- = 0.7 grade points
F = 0 grade points
P (pass), NP (not pass), I (incomplete), W (withdrawal) will be ignored.

Departments at UET Lahore use a GPA system based on letter grades such as A, B, C, D, and F, in line with the HEC grading policy. However, grading criteria may vary slightly between departments. For example, what percentage qualifies as an A or B can differ. UET does not typically use intermediate grades like A+ or B-, but some departments may assign different weightages to specific courses, especially core or lab-intensive subjects. This GPA calculator takes such variations into account by letting you assign credit hours to each course, which reflect the course’s importance or "weight" in your overall GPA.
